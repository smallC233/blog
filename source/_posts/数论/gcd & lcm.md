# GCD 和 LCM

设: $a = p_1^{c_1}·p_2^{c_2}\dots\ ·p_m^{c_m}$ ，

​    $b = p_1^{f_1}·p_2^{f_2}\dots\ ·p_m^{f_m}$

那么：$gcd(a, b) = p_1^{min(c_1, f_1)}·p_2^{min(c_2, f_2)}\dots\ ·p_m^{min(c_m, f_m)}$

​     $lcm(a, b) = p_1^{max(c_1, f_1)}·p_2^{max(c_2, f_2)}\dots\ ·p_m^{max(c_m, f_m)}$
